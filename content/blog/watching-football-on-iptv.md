## How to avoid buffering during matches

Most buffering is fixable, and often it's at your end:

- **Go wired.** An Ethernet adapter for your Firestick or box is the single biggest upgrade for live sport.
- **Check your speed.** 10 Mbps for HD, 25 Mbps+ for 4K.
- **Use 5GHz Wi-Fi** and sit near the router if you can't go wired.
- **Restart your device** before a big match to clear its memory.
- **Pick a reliable provider** — anti-buffer servers and high uptime do the heavy lifting.
